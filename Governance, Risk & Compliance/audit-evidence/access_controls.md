# Audit Evidence: Access Controls

## Control Objective

Access is authorized, least-privilege, and regularly reviewed.

## Evidence Sources

- IAM role and policy baselines
- MFA enforcement evidence
- Privileged access review records
- Joiner/mover/leaver process artifacts
- Access anomaly detection reports

## Periodic Checks

- Quarterly access review completion
- Privileged role attestation
- Over-permissive policy remediation tracking
