# Audit Evidence: Encryption Controls

## Control Objective

Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit using approved cryptographic controls.

## Evidence Sources

- KMS key policies and rotation settings
- Service-level encryption configurations (S3, EBS, RDS, etc.)
- TLS policy/baseline documentation
- Exceptions and risk acceptance records (if any)

## Periodic Checks

- Key usage and rotation review
- Unencrypted resource detection reports
