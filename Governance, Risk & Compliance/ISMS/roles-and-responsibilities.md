# Roles and Responsibilities

## Governance Roles

| Role | Key Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| CISO / Security Executive | Approves security policy, risk appetite, and major risk acceptance decisions |
| Security Governance Lead | Maintains ISMS, coordinates audits, manages control framework alignment |
| Platform Security Engineering | Designs and operates technical controls; maintains security baselines |
| Risk Owner (Business/Engineering) | Owns identified risks, treatment plans, and acceptance timelines |
| Control Owner | Maintains specific control design and operational effectiveness |
| Internal Audit / Assurance | Performs independent review and evidence validation |

## RACI Summary

- Policy maintenance: Governance Lead (R), CISO (A)
- Risk assessment execution: Governance Lead + Risk Owners (R), CISO (A)
- Control implementation: Platform Security Engineering (R), Control Owner (A)
- Audit evidence readiness: Control Owners (R), Governance Lead (A)
