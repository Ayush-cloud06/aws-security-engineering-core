# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

Define minimum information security requirements for systems and data managed by this platform.

## Policy Statements

- Access to systems and data must follow least privilege and need-to-know principles.
- Strong authentication and centralized identity governance are mandatory.
- Sensitive data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Security logging and monitoring must be enabled for all production workloads.
- Security events must be triaged, tracked, and responded to using documented procedures.
- Changes to production infrastructure must be controlled and auditable.
- Vulnerabilities must be identified, prioritized, and remediated based on risk.
- Third-party services must be evaluated for security and compliance impact.

## Compliance

Non-compliance with this policy must be documented, risk accepted by an authorized owner, and tracked through remediation.

## Review Cadence

- Review frequency: annual or upon material change
- Policy owner: Security Governance Lead
- Approver: CISO
